Trading Bot

Project Architecture

A semi-autonomous algorithmic trading bot for an Alpaca paper account. Trades on validated daily-bar momentum signals across the full liquid US equity + crypto universe, with multi-source intelligence (FRED macro, Massive news + sentiment, GDELT events, SEC filings, Truth Social VIPs) and a hard pre-trade risk-gate layer.

Paper-only enforced Empirical PF 1.25 / Sharpe 0.76 Backtest-validated

What the bot actually does #

One sentence: every trading day, it ranks the full liquid US equity + crypto universe, classifies the market regime, and (only in trending_up) places long-only bracket orders on names that pass a momentum-indicator screen and a six-rule risk gate.

It does not day-trade or scalp. Holding period is days-to-weeks. Indicators all run on daily bars (RSI 14, MACD, EMA 20). Intraday cadence (intel-scan every hour during market hours; crypto-scan hourly 24/7) exists to catch signal flips and place orders, not to chase intraday moves.

It does not sell short, trade options, hedge, or use margin. Long-only on stocks and USD-quoted crypto pairs. Position sizing is currently flat 0.5% per-trade risk; concentration capped at 4.5% of equity per name.

Safety guarantees #

  • Paper-only enforced at config load. Settings.bot_mode is a Literal["paper", "live"] with a validator that raises LiveModeDisabled if anything but "paper" is set. Cannot be silently flipped.
  • Every order goes through the same risk gate. RiskManager.check() is called inline by the orchestrator and the simulator. No bypass path; raising RiskRuleViolation is the only way to fail-closed, and the orchestrator catches and skips, not retries.
  • Crypto orders are post-fill verified. After the non-atomic market entry → stop sequence, the bot re-queries Alpaca within 500ms; if the position is naked, it market-flattens immediately.
  • Hourly stop sweep on stocks. A scheduled job re-checks every open position has a live stop order; if any are missing, an alert email fires.
  • Halt fires on a -2% daily / -5% weekly P&L. Once halted, no new entries fire (existing brackets continue to manage themselves). Halt clears at next trading day's open.

Universe selection #

The bot looks at the entire US tradable market via the screener, but trades only the top of the ranked list.

Stage 1 — universe build (Massive-first, post-Plan-6)

  1. massive.daily_grouped(date) — one call returns OHLC + volume for ~10,000 US equities for the most-recent trading day.
  2. Intersect with alpaca.get_active_assets("us_equity") — only keeps symbols we can actually place orders on.
  3. Apply liquidity filter: stocks need price ≥ $10 AND ADV ≥ $10M; crypto bypasses both (Alpaca paper crypto volume is sandbox-level and doesn't reflect real market depth).
  4. Per-ticker bars are fetched only for the top-200-by-ADV pre-shortlist — keeps Alpaca rate-limit usage in budget.

Stage 1 score

composite = one_day_return × 1.4
          + (5d_return − SPY_5d_return)        # relative strength
          + min(volume_ratio, 3.0) × 2.0       # volume surge

Top 100 by composite go to Stage 2.

Stage 2 — lane endorsement

LaneConditions
MomentumRSI 55-70, MACD > signal, close > EMA20, 5d return > 0
Mean-reversionRSI < 30 AND price < lower Bollinger Band (2σ, 20d)
Breakoutclose > 20-day high AND volume > 1.5× 20d avg

Symbols endorsed by ≥1 lane are sorted by max conviction across lanes and written to strategy/opportunities.md.

Regime detection #

Module: regime.py. Pure function of SPY bars + optional VIX.

RegimeTrigger (in priority order)
risk_offVIX > 28 (if VIX feed available), OR 20d realised vol > 22% (configurable), OR 20d drawdown < -10%
sidewaysVIX in (22, 28] caps regime here even if SPY trends up; OR mixed EMA signals
trending_upSPY close > EMA50 > EMA200, vol < 25%, VIX ≤ 22
trending_downSPY close < EMA50 < EMA200

VIX comes from FRED's VIXCLS series (free, no key). 10Y yield and Fed Funds also pulled but only used in the dashboard "Macro" panel today.

Entry strategy #

Module: strategy.py. After the Plan 5b backtest verdict (mean-reversion lost 0.66 PF in sideways across 49 trades), only momentum-in-trending_up is enabled.

MomentumStrategy.evaluate

  1. RSI(14) ∈ [55, 70] — uptrend strength but not overbought
  2. MACD > MACD signal — bullish crossover state
  3. close > EMA(20) — price above short-term trend
  4. 5d return > 0 — recent direction confirmed
  5. Compute stop: max(EMA20, entry × 0.95) — whichever is closer to entry
  6. Compute qty: min(0.5%-equity-risk / per_share_risk, 4.5%-equity-cap / entry)

If qty rounds down to less than 1 share, returns HOLD. Otherwise BUY signal.

Other regimes

strategy_for_regime() returns None for trending_down / sideways / risk_off. The orchestrator interprets None as "skip entries this regime; existing positions still managed by their bracket orders." This is a deliberate choice from Plan 5b — losing slices got removed rather than band-aided.

Pre-trade checks #

Six rules in RiskManager.check(). Every entry passes them; RiskRuleViolation is the only fail mode.

RuleConditionSource
haltedRiskState.halted == Falsecircuit-breaker (daily/weekly P&L)
per_trade_risk_pct(entry − stop) × qty / equity ≤ 1.0%config.risk.per_trade_risk_pct
max_position_pctentry × qty / equity ≤ 10%config.risk.max_position_pct
max_symbol_concentration_pctexisting + new notional / equity ≤ 5%config.risk.max_symbol_concentration_pct

Plus, after Plan 6c, an optional news-sentiment gate sits between the BUY signal and the risk check: when config.strategy.sentiment_floor is set, entries on names with an aggregate news sentiment below the floor are skipped (defaults to disabled until the backtest sweep finds a value that improves PF).

Execution #

Stocks — bracket orders (atomic)

One BracketOrderRequest to Alpaca with three legs: market entry, stop-loss, take-profit. All three placed atomically by Alpaca's matching engine. Take-profit is set at entry + 2 × per_share_risk (2:1 reward-to-risk).

Crypto — market entry + standalone stop, post-fill verified

Alpaca crypto doesn't support brackets, so it's two non-atomic API calls: market entry (fills near-instantly), then a standalone stop order. Between the two, a flash crash could leave us naked. Mitigation: 500ms after both legs are submitted, the client re-queries Alpaca; if a position exists but no live stop order is on the symbol, it market-flattens the position immediately. (Module: alpaca_client.py::_verify_crypto_stop_or_flatten.)

Continuous stop verification

A scheduled job (bot verify-stops, every :20 / :50 24/7) sweeps every open position, confirms each has a live stop order, and emails an alert if any are naked. Catches the rare bracket-leg detachment bug Alpaca has had in the past.

While in position #

  • Bracket sits in Alpaca's book. No bot intervention required between fill and exit; all three legs (entry, stop, take-profit) are in the order book.
  • No trailing stops. The Plan 5b backtest tested 6 trailing-stop configs and every variant underperformed the no-trailing baseline. Code path exists but disabled by default.
  • Portfolio-watch alerts. Every 30 min during market hours, the bot snapshots positions and emails on any >2% intraday move (in either direction).
  • Stop-leg verification. Every :20 / :50 24/7, the verify-stops sweep confirms every open position has a corresponding stop order. Naked positions trigger an immediate email.
  • Existing positions stay open through regime changes. When the regime shifts to risk_off or sideways, no new entries fire, but already-filled positions continue to be managed by their bracket orders.

Halt mechanics #

RiskState.halted is recomputed at the start of every scan. It flips to True on either:

  • daily_pnl_pct ≤ -2.0 (config.risk.daily_loss_limit_pct)
  • weekly_pnl_pct ≤ -5.0 (config.risk.weekly_loss_limit_pct)

While halted, RiskManager.check() raises immediately on every BUY signal — no entries fire. Existing bracket orders continue to manage their positions independently.

Halt clears at the next scan after midnight UTC (daily roll). Plan 5e (deferred) will add soft-halt at -1.5% (no new entries, no manual reset) and intraday equity polling for finer granularity.

Alerting #

All alerts go to the email address in config.email.to (Gmail SMTP via app password). Alert types:

  • Trade event — order placed or rejected by risk manager (intel-scan)
  • Big-move portfolio alert — any open position ±2% intraday (portfolio-watch)
  • Naked-position alert — any open position lacks a live stop order (verify-stops)
  • VIP tweet HIGH-severity — Truth Social keyword match (vip-scan)
  • Midday rich-report — comprehensive 12:30 ET HTML report
  • EOD rich-report — comprehensive 16:30 ET HTML report (regime + intel + positions, no scan side-effect)

Backtest verdict #

The backtest harness (bot backtest) replays the real strategy + risk_manager + orchestrator code paths against historical bars. Reuses MomentumStrategy.evaluate, RiskManager.check, and detect_regime_from_bars verbatim — only the day-by-day clock loop, the bracket-leg simulator, and the metric aggregator are new code.

Acceptance gate

  1. Overall Sharpe ≥ 0.5
  2. At least one strategy in the dominant regime has trades ≥ 30 AND PF ≥ 1.0

Evolution loop #

Weekly job (bot evolve, Sat 10am) reads data/closed_trades.db, computes per-strategy stats (win rate, profit factor, total P&L), and proposes parameter changes via a Kelly-conservative algorithm: bad runs cut per_trade_risk_pct by 50%, good runs raise by 25%. Proposals are applied to strategy/params.yaml only with --apply; otherwise just logged to strategy/rules.md.

Currently only tunes per_trade_risk_pct and stop_pct. Plan 5f (deferred) extends to RSI thresholds, MACD parameters, and regime-conditional metrics.

Known gaps #

  • No earnings-window exclusion. Plan 5c-4 deferred — Massive's earnings endpoint is paid. Finnhub free-tier integration is the next planned vendor.
  • No soft halt or intraday halt. Plan 5e deferred — current halt is daily-close based and clears next day.
  • No conviction-based sizing. Every trade gets flat 0.5% risk. Plan 5d deferred.
  • No options trading. Regime allocations reserve % for options but nothing trades them.
  • No live news-sentiment filter. Module is built and cached, but config.strategy.sentiment_floor defaults to None until a backtest sweep finds a threshold that improves PF.
  • Rate-limited Massive plan. Free-tier limits force sequential calls; bot rank needs to budget around them.
  • Daily-bar only. No intraday strategies. Indicators recompute as the latest bar updates, but signal flips happen on hourly+ timescales.
